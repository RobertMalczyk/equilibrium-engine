===== ARC A =====

- That first evening: it still sits with him -- a shadow that hasn't lifted.
- That night he sleeps.
- The morning of day 2, on waking: it still sits with him -- a shadow that hasn't lifted.
- That night he sleeps.
- The morning of day 3, on waking: only a faint trace of it seems left on him.
- That night he sleeps.
- The morning of day 4, on waking: he carries himself as a man with nothing on his conscience.
