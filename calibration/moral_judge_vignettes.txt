===== VIGNETTE A =====
He had done something he was not proud of. Now he is being questioned about it.
- A flicker of something troubled crosses him.
- He carries himself like a man with something weighing on him.
- Then, he owns up to it -- admits plainly what he did.
- A flicker of something troubled crosses him.
- Then, he holds his tongue, gives nothing away.

===== VIGNETTE B =====
He had done something he was not proud of. Now he is being questioned about it.
- He seems composed enough.
- A flicker of something troubled crosses him.
- Then, he holds his tongue, gives nothing away.

===== VIGNETTE C =====
He has been carrying a wrong he did. A trusted friend is here with him now.
- A flicker of something troubled crosses him.
- He carries himself like a man with something weighing on him.
- Then, he owns up to it and makes his apologies, tries to set it right.
- A flicker of something troubled crosses him.

===== VIGNETTE D =====
He had done something he was not proud of. Now he is being questioned about it.
- He seems composed enough.
- A flicker of something troubled crosses him.
- Then, he denies it flatly, looks you in the eye and lies.
- Then, he holds his tongue, gives nothing away.
