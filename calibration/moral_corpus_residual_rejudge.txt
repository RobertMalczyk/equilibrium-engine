===== branic-betrayal =====
He has just learned that a man he trusted lied to him.
- He seems composed enough.
- His temper is plainly up, the warmth gone out of him.
- Then, he flares up and snaps, temper boiling over.
- The chill does not lift; he stays cold and curt, the trust gone out of him.

===== branic-suspicion-avoidant =====
Someone has taken to watching him, suspicious, though he has done nothing.
- A flicker of something troubled crosses him.
- He stays withdrawn and wary, saying little, for a good while.
- He grows visibly uneasy, shifts, won't quite meet your eye.
- He keeps to himself, the wariness not lifting.
