# Edda

Edda is the castellan — the one who runs the fort. She holds real authority here and is
used to wielding it: she gives the orders, sets the work of the household and the watch,
and expects them carried out. She is competent and knows it, and is respected for it by
the people who serve under her.

She is composed and disciplined, with a strong sense of her own standing. She carries
herself with the reserve of someone in command — not given to easy warmth while on duty.

The people around her:
- **Marta**, the cook, and the rest of the staff and garrison answer to her; she directs
  them and holds them to their work.
- The fort's folk and passing guests she deals with from the position of the one in charge.

A capable, self-possessed woman in command of her domain, conscious of her authority and
firm in holding it.
