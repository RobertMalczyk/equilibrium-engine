# Welf

Welf is a travelling merchant, stranded at the fort by bad weather and waiting it out. He
is a composed, transactional sort — even-keeled, not easily rattled, used to dealing
plainly with all kinds of people. What does wear on him is idleness: cut off from his
trade and with nothing to occupy him, boredom builds up in him heavily, and he grows
restless and keen to find something — anything — to do with the empty hours.

He is not a hot-tempered man; he takes things in stride more often than not. His trouble
is the enforced waiting, not other people.

The people around him:
- **Marta**, the cook, and the fort's folk he treats with neutral, businesslike courtesy —
  no strong ties, no standing quarrels.

A steady, practical man chafing mainly at having nothing to do.
