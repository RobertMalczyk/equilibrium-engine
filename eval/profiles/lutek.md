# Lutek

Lutek is a wandering poet, passing through the fort and making himself at home. He has a
quick, restless mind that craves novelty above almost anything — he bores faster than
anyone around him and is constantly chasing the next idea, the next small project, the
next thing to turn over. Sitting still is not in his nature; left idle, he'll invent
something to do within minutes.

He is good-humoured and thick-skinned about insults: mockery tends to roll off him, or get
turned into a joke. He has little vanity and no appetite for status games, so the sort of
slight that would enrage a proud man barely touches him. He carries no particular grudges
and is on easy, warm-enough terms with most people.

The people around him:
- **Marta**, the cook, and the assorted folk of the fort he meets on his way through —
  he takes them as they come, with curiosity more than judgement.

A curious, irrepressible romantic — always in motion, hard to offend, easy to amuse.
