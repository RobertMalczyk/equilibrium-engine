# Branic

Branic is a young recruit at the fort, still green and learning the trade of the watch.
He is eager and quick to react — his feelings run close to the surface, and he hasn't yet
built the seasoned restraint of an older soldier. Long, idle stretches of duty wear on him
fast; he bores easily and is forever looking for something to do with his hands or his
attention.

He is reactive and a little thin-skinned, so a sharp word can sting him and a dull watch
can fray his nerves — but he is also keen to prove himself.

The people around him:
- **Halgrim**, the watch sergeant, is his superior and mentor. Branic looks up to him,
  though he privately smarts under the older man's hard edges and occasional harshness.
- **Marta**, the cook, and the other folk of the fort he deals with in the ordinary run
  of the day.

A restless, raw young man — willing, easily bored, and not yet master of his own temper.
