# A restless afternoon, with nothing

The same wound-up man, in a bare cell of a day — nothing to find, nowhere to put himself.

Observable record (time in ticks; only what a bystander could see):

- t=0: he prowls about, looking for something to do
- t=21: he circles the yard again, finding nothing
- t=42: he picks things up and puts them down, jaw working
- t=63: he stalks the walls once more, emptier-handed each round
